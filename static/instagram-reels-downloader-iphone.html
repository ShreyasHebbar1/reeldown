How to Download Instagram Reels on iPhone — Full iOS Guide

Method 1: Using Safari on iPhone (Recommended)

Safari is the best browser for downloading files on iPhone because it integrates directly with the Files app and allows you to save videos to your Camera Roll. Here are the exact steps:

  1. Open Instagram and copy the link: Find the public Reel you want to save. Tap the Share icon (the paper airplane) below the post, then tap Copy Link. The Instagram Reel URL is now on your clipboard.
  2. Go to VidsDownloader in Safari: Open Safari and navigate to VidsDownloader.in. Tap the input box and paste the URL. The tool immediately detects the link and starts fetching the video.
  3. Confirm the preview: Within a few seconds, a preview card appears showing the thumbnail, creator username, and video duration. Confirm it is the correct Reel.
  4. Tap Download Video: Tap the green Download Video button. Safari will show a pop-up at the top of the screen: "Do you want to download video.mp4?" — tap Download.
  5. Track your download: A small download indicator (circular arrow) appears in Safari's address bar. Once it fills completely, the download is finished.

Where Is the Downloaded Reel on iPhone?

Safari saves downloaded files to the Downloads folder inside the Files app. Here is how to find and move your video to the Camera Roll:

  1. Open the Files app on your iPhone (it comes pre-installed — search for it if you can't find it).
  2. Tap Browse at the bottom, then tap Downloads under iCloud Drive or On My iPhone.
  3. Your downloaded Reel will appear as a .mp4 file. Tap it once to play it directly in the Files app.
  4. To move it to your Camera Roll (Photos app): press and hold the file, then tap Share → Save Video. It will appear immediately in your Photos app under Recents.

Method 2: Using Chrome on iPhone

Google Chrome on iPhone works almost identically to Safari for downloading files. The key difference is that Chrome saves files to its own internal storage within the Files app. Here are the steps:

  1. Open Chrome on your iPhone and go to VidsDownloader.in.
  2. Copy your Instagram Reel link from the Instagram app and paste it into VidsDownloader.
  3. Tap Download Video when the preview appears. Chrome will automatically begin downloading the file.
  4. To find the file: tap the ⋮ menu in Chrome → Downloads. Your video will be listed there.
  5. To move to Camera Roll: tap the share icon next to the file → Save Video.
